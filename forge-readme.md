# Forge

**Where you're built.**

Forge is a personal accountability platform for people serious about closing the gap between who they are and who they're trying to become. It's not a habit tracker, not a journal, and not another generic productivity suite — it's a system for making commitments with real weight, understanding why your plans actually break, and extracting real lessons from your own experience.

## What it does

Forge is built around one core loop:

**Commit → Track → Debrief → Challenge**

- **Commit** — Make commitments with real structure: what you're doing, why it matters, what "done" looks like, and what it costs to break it.
- **Track** — Log friction the moment something derails your plan. Over time, Forge surfaces the patterns in what keeps breaking your momentum.
- **Debrief** — A structured reflection after every commitment ends. Lessons accumulate automatically into a personal Playbook built entirely from your own experience.
- **Challenge** — Bring an idea or decision before committing to it. Forge stress-tests it like a rigorous skeptic, surfacing the assumptions and risks you haven't considered.

It also includes an AI-powered onboarding interview that learns who you are before you see a single feature, a personalized dashboard, and a social layer (Accountability Circles) built around quiet, intentional witness rather than public performance.

## Tech stack

- **Next.js 14** (App Router) + **TypeScript**
- **Tailwind CSS**
- **Zustand** for state management
- **Anthropic API (Claude)** for the onboarding interview, friction pattern insights, debrief synthesis, and idea stress-testing

Actively in development. Core modules and the AI onboarding flow are built; social layer and further polish in progress.
